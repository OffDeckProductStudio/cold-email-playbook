## B2B SaaS

### Overview

- Fast-moving, competitive market
- Always optimizing for growth metrics
- Tech-savvy, high expectations

### Common Pain Points

1. **Scaling Outbound Without Losing Quality**
   - Description: As they grow, personalization suffers
   - Impact: Lower reply rates, wasted SDR time
   - Their language: "We can't scale without it becoming spammy"
   - Our solution: [Your solution]

2. **Long Sales Cycles**
   - Description: Enterprise deals take 6+ months
   - Impact: Cash flow challenges, forecasting difficulty
   - Their language: "Deals keep slipping"
   - Our solution: [Your solution]

3. **SDR Ramp Time**
   - Description: New reps take 3-6 months to be productive
   - Impact: Missed quotas, high turnover cost
   - Their language: "We can't get reps productive fast enough"
   - Our solution: [Your solution]

4. **Attribution and Reporting**
   - Description: Can't track what's working
   - Impact: Wasted budget, wrong decisions
   - Their language: "We don't know what's driving pipeline"
   - Our solution: [Your solution]

### Trigger Events

- Series A/B/C funding announced
- Hiring multiple SDRs/AEs
- New VP of Sales or CRO
- Expansion into new market
- Competitor acquisition

### Key Metrics

- MRR/ARR growth
- CAC payback period
- Sales cycle length
- Quota attainment
- Pipeline coverage

### Sample Opening Lines

- "Saw you're hiring 5 SDRs — scaling outbound is exciting but tricky to do without quality dropping."
- "Congrats on the Series B. Most sales leaders I talk to at this stage are wrestling with..."
