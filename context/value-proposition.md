# Value Proposition

Define what you offer and why prospects should care. This context helps AI generate compelling, benefit-focused email copy.

---

## Instructions

1. Complete each section with your actual product/service details
2. Focus on outcomes and benefits, not just features
3. Include specific numbers and proof points where possible
4. Update as your positioning evolves

---

## Core Value Proposition

### One-Liner

```
[Your Company] helps [target audience] achieve [primary outcome] 
by [how you do it differently].
```

**Example:**

```
Acme helps B2B sales teams book 3x more meetings by automating 
personalized outreach at scale.
```

## Proof Points

### Quantified Results

Specific numbers from customer success:

```
- [Customer A] saw [X% improvement] in [metric] within [timeframe]
- [Customer B] achieved [specific result] after [timeframe]
- Average customer sees [X result] in [Y time]
```

**Examples:**

```
- TechCorp increased meetings booked by 340% in 90 days
- Average customer sees 5x ROI within 6 months
- Customers report 80% reduction in time spent on manual outreach
```
